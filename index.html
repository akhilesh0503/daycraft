Why interest-first?

Task lists make you dread your day. Daycraft starts from what you actually look forward to, then fills the rest around it.

Reading, gym, coding, music — anything. Add your mood and energy. That's it.

AI weaves your day

Powered by Groq + Llama 3.3 70B. 2–3 seconds, free tier. Falls back to a smart rule-based schedule if you skip the API key.
